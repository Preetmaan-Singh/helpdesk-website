Fiscal Year 2025 Internal Help Desk Operations Plan

By Preetmaan Singh Brar (HD Supervisor)

Reason for the Plan

Hello, I am Preetmaan Singh Brar, Help Desk Supervisor. This Operations Plan provides an overview of our upcoming fiscal year goals and strategies to improve support efficiency, service reliability, and user satisfaction.

Mission

Our mission is to deliver fast, reliable, and customer-focused IT support. User benefit: Faster resolution and reduced downtime for employees.

Vision

Our vision is to build a proactive, data-driven help desk that anticipates user needs and continuously improves through innovation and training, ensuring seamless IT operations in the future.

SMART Goals for FY 2025

Goal 1: Improve First Contact Resolution Rate

Increase FCR by 8% by December 31, 2025 (HDI Metric 6.1)

User benefit: Faster one-call solutions reduce downtime and frustration.

Goal 2: Reduce Resolution Time for Priority 2 Issues

Reduce MTTR by 12% by December 31, 2025 (HDI Metric 7.2)

User benefit: Quicker fixes mean less business disruption.

Goal 3: Improve Customer Satisfaction Scores

Increase CSAT score by 5% by December 31, 2025 (Metric 8.1)

User benefit: Better communication and care lead to happier users.

Goal 4: Improve Knowledge Base Utilization

Boost knowledge base article use by 15% by December 31, 2025 (Metric 6.3)

User benefit: Self-service allows quick solutions without waiting for support.

Goal 5: Improve SLA Compliance

Increase SLA compliance from 85% to 92% by December 31, 2025 (Metric 6.2)

User benefit: Reliable and consistent service that users can trust.
